In 2020, about 928 million people faced severe food insecurity, an increase of 148 million from 2019. Food insecurity among women was found to be 10% higher than among men in 2020. Most of the world's undernourished people are located in Asia and Africa. Balochistan and Sindh have the highest number of calorie deficient households. Food and nutrition security have been given top priority in the governments development programs from 2015 to 2025.
