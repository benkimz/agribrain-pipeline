My Ministry has developed nine flagships that serve as the core of its 10-year Agricultural Sector Growth and Transformation Strategy (ASTGS). This strategy details the flagship projects prioritized for implementation within the first five years. An additional set of projects will be developed for the next five years of the strategy to match the transformation needs at the time. President Uhuru Kenyatta and Deputy President William Ruto are thanked for their vision to provide access to affordable and nutritious food to every Kenyan.
