There are no large scale fruit growers in Uganda at the moment. Current production is estimated at 5000 acres (2000ha) on 2500 smallholdings in Luwero and Kayunga. Most of the current exports have been in raw fruits and vegetables. The project design is hinged on a core investor joint venture with Government and out-growers within the catchment area of the estate. It is being designed to support GOUs Plan for Modernisation of Agriculture aimed at increasing productivity and incomes of farmers.
