Training plays an important role for the change in behavior by improvement of knowledge, skill and ability of farmers. Potato Seed Tuber Production Techniques Manual prepared under the JICA project on Rehabilitation and Recovery from Nepal Earthquake (RRNE) is useful for rural Farmers and technicians related to Agriculture offices for the training material. The International Food Policy Research Institute predicted a bleak future for the world food situation.
