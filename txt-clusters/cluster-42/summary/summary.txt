The research was carried out at Wedarijaksa station, Trangkil Sugar Mill area, Pati, Central Javain 20192021. Four sugarcane varieties were grown using a double rows system. The decline in tonnes of cane and sugar per hectare between PC and RC1 decreased by 22.7% and 21.0%, respectively, for AAS Agribun, ASAAgribun and CMG Agribin. AMS Agriben showed the smallest decrease in tonnes per hectar of cane (4%), and increase in tonnes a hectare (2%). The highest number of tonnes of sugar in PC was achieved by ASA agribun (12.8 t ha1).
