Women play a significant role in almost all aspects of agricultural activities such as crop production, animal husbandry, domestic and other income generating activities. The Aira woreda is located in western part of Ethiopia in western wollega zone in Oromiyaa region. It is located 504 km from Addis Ababa and 173 km from Nekemte. Most of the people in the study area are Protestants, Orthodox, Muslim, Adventist or follow other religions.
