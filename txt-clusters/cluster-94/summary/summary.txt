Aquaculture is promoted to sustain livelihood and improve food security in Sub-Saharan Africa. However, the production in SSA is still low at less than 1% of the total global production. Poor water quality is cited to be one of the factors limiting the growth of the aquaculture sector. A survey was carried out among sh farmers in vedistricts of the Rwenzori region. It found that 81% and 80% had poor knowledge and practices concerning water quality in Aquaculture. Many farmers using earthen ponds abandoned the activity.
